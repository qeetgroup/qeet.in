Qeet ID, the first venture inside Qeet Group, has begun discovery — the early phase of figuring out exactly what to build, for whom, and in what order.

Qeet ID started from a question every engineering team eventually asks: why is authentication this expensive, this complicated, and this divided between "simple developer tools" and "real enterprise platforms"? Modern teams ship across both worlds simultaneously, but the auth tooling that is actually available either trades simplicity for depth or depth for simplicity. We do not believe that trade is permanent.

The product will be a single authentication and authorization platform — SSO, MFA, passkeys, RBAC, stateful sessions — that teams can drop in early and grow into as they scale. The point is the same product feels right at every stage, with no migration when scale arrives.

The discovery phase will run through the summer. We will be talking to engineering teams across startups, scale-ups, and enterprises about what they actually need from auth tooling — and what the current generation of platforms still gets wrong.

If you are running auth at a team that is growing fast and you have strong opinions, we would like to hear them. Write to [support@qeet.in](mailto:support@qeet.in).
