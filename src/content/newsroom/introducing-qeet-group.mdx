Today we are announcing Qeet Group — a multi-company holding built on a single philosophy: that meaningful progress begins with the right question, asked early, and the patience to chase the answer for years.

Most great ventures fail not because the execution was wrong, but because the original question was too small. Qeet Group is being built to back the opposite — companies that take long-term bets on questions worth answering, with the operating discipline to ship work they can be proud of along the way.

We are organized as a holding structure deliberately. Subsidiaries share a philosophy, a quality standard, and a refusal to ship work the team cannot defend. They do not share a roadmap, an org chart, or a thesis. Each venture is built for what it is.

The work we want to fund is the work that is hard to fund elsewhere — products that need a few years before they look like products, and teams that need an operating environment that values the question more than the quarter.

There will be more to share as the first ventures take shape. Until then, you can find us at [qeet.in](/).
