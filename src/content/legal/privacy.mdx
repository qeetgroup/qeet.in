This policy describes what information Qeet Group ("we", "us") collects when you use our website at qeet.in, and how we handle that information.

> This document is a placeholder draft. It reflects how the site is currently configured, but it has not been reviewed by counsel. Replace before launch.

## What we collect

We collect very little, and only what is necessary to operate the site and respond to direct inquiries.

- **Contact submissions.** When you send us a message through the contact form, we collect the name, email address, topic, and message body you provide. We use this information to reply to you. We do not add it to a marketing list.
- **Analytics.** We use a privacy-focused analytics service (Plausible) to understand which pages are visited and how often. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. The data we see is aggregate.
- **Server logs.** Our hosting provider keeps standard request logs (IP, user agent, timestamp) for short periods as part of normal operations.

We do not use advertising trackers, third-party social pixels, or session replay tools.

## How we use information

We use the information described above to:

- Respond to messages you send us.
- Operate the site and keep it running reliably.
- Understand which content is useful so we can improve it.

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third parties except as needed to operate the services listed below.

## Third-party services

We rely on a small number of services to run the site:

- **Vercel** for hosting.
- **Resend** for transactional email delivery when you submit the contact form.
- **Plausible** for privacy-friendly analytics.

Each of these processes some data on our behalf, governed by their respective terms.
