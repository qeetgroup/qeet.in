## Overview

Qeet ID is the first venture inside Qeet Group. It started from a question every engineering team eventually asks: why is authentication this expensive, this complicated, and this divided between "simple developer tools" and "real enterprise platforms"? Qeet ID was built to close that gap — developer simplicity on the surface, enterprise depth underneath, at a price that does not force teams to choose between the two.

## What they build

A single authentication and authorization platform that ships as a drop-in SDK and React component set. The capability surface covers the work modern teams actually need: SSO (SAML 2.0, OIDC) across 50+ identity providers, MFA (TOTP, SMS, email OTP) and passkeys for phishing-resistant sign-in, RBAC and ABAC for fine-grained access control, stateful sessions, multi-tenant isolation, and audit-ready logging. Everything sits on a single identity graph, so policy decisions are coherent across products and tenants.

SDKs are available for TypeScript, Go, Python, Rust, React, Next.js, and mobile. The drop-in components mean a team can stand up production sign-in in days, not quarters.

## Who they serve

Developers building their first product. Startups adding their first compliance certifications. Enterprises consolidating identity infrastructure across business units. The point is the same product feels right at every stage — no migration when scale arrives.
